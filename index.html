About Me

I have lived in Curitiba, London, Lisbon, Cologne and Montreal. In every role and on every project, I bring a blend of logical reasoning, analysis, and curiosity which enables me to locate pain points and develop improvements. I am driven by feedback, whether from clients, team members or managers and value open communication, which I have found crucial in bridging cultural differences and working and volunteering in different environments and on varied initiatives.

Bringing my curiosity to every project and every position, I am looking to work in an environment where I can use data to better understand complex processes and make decisions to affect growth and positive change.
